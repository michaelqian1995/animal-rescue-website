Oscar

Oscar has a happy, easy going temperament and a real sweet heart. He loves playing with squeaker toys and will be your best friend if you offer him a treat. Oscar will need some training to help him correct his strong lead manners but other than that he really adores a daily walk to keep him fit & happy.

Bindhi

Bindhi is a happy sweetheart and she greets everyone with a big, gorgeous smile. Bindhi loves to please and she is very trainable. Bindhi is already an awesome girl, however to help Bindhi reach her full potential she will need an experienced, calm handler who will invest time into correcting her lead manners, excitable nipping and provide ongoing dog socialisation.
